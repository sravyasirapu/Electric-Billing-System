General Questions

How do I calculate my electricity bill?
You can use our Electricity Bill Calculator page by entering your consumed units to see an instant estimate.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods including UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Netbanking. You can pay on our Make Payment page.
How can I download my bill?
Navigate to the View Bill / History page. You will find options to view and download past bills.
Who do I contact for incorrect billing?
Please visit our Support page for contact details or file a ticket on the Service Requests page.
